What are SHAP values? SHAP (SHapley Additive exPlanations) measures how each feature contributes to the model's prediction. A higher |SHAP value| means the feature has more impact on predictions. Positive mean SHAP values push predictions higher; negative values push them lower. Works with both Neural Network (KernelExplainer) and XGBoost (TreeExplainer) models.
